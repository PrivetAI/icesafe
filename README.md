# Ice Safe For Fishermans

Ice Safe For Fishermans is a mobile application designed to help ice fishing enthusiasts stay safe on frozen water bodies. The app provides comprehensive tools for assessing ice thickness and determining whether it is safe to walk, fish, or bring equipment onto the ice. With real-time safety calculations and clear visual indicators, anglers can make informed decisions before venturing onto potentially dangerous ice surfaces.

## Features

- **Safety Calculator** — Calculate the maximum safe load based on ice thickness and ice type. The app provides clear danger, warning, and safe indicators to help you understand the current conditions at a glance.

- **Reference Table** — Access recommended ice thickness values for various activities and equipment, from walking on foot to bringing vehicles onto the ice.

- **Safety Guide** — Learn essential safety tips, emergency procedures, and best practices for ice fishing to minimise risks.

- **Measurement Log** — Keep a history of your ice thickness measurements across different locations and dates to track conditions over time.

- **Ice Formation Guide** — Understand how ice forms, strengthens, and weakens under different weather conditions to better predict safety levels.
